🎯 FUN FACTS & TRIVIA

  • Money Heist is originally a Spanish show titled "La Casa de Papel" (The House of Paper).
  • The series was produced by Álex de la Iglesia and Esther Martínez Sobral.
  • The red jumpsuits and Dalí masks became iconic symbols of the series worldwide.
  • The show was initially aired on Spanish television before becoming a Netflix global phenomenon.
  • Character names are taken from Spanish cities: Tokyo, Rio, Berlin, Nairobi, Denver, and Lisbon.
  • The series was one of Netflix's most-watched non-English language shows.
  • The theme music is a remixed version of "Bella Ciao," an Italian folk protest song.
  • The Professor's real name is Sergio Marquina, revealed in the series.
  • Multiple heists are planned throughout the 5 seasons, each more complex than the last.
  • The show has won numerous awards including Spanish Academy Awards and international recognition.
